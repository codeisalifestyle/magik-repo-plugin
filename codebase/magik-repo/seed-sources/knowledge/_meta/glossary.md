# Glossary

Canonical terms used across the project. When a term appears in more than one domain with the same meaning, define it here once and link from the domain entries.

## Conventions

- Alphabetical.
- One paragraph per term, plus optional links.
- A term joins the glossary only after it has appeared in **≥ 2 domains**, or has been the subject of misunderstanding (write a `fieldnote` first; promote to glossary if the confusion recurs).
