# Commitments

Short-lived, dated follow-ups. Read at session start; pruned when due or resolved.

> **Not durable.** Commitments are the opposite of `knowledge/`. They expire. If a commitment leads to a durable lesson or decision, distill it into the KB; the commitment itself still ages out.

## Format

```markdown
- [ ] YYYY-MM-DD · <domain> · <one-line description> · scope: <paths or none>
```

- The leading `[ ]` / `[x]` is the status. Mark `[x]` when satisfied.
- The first date is the `due` date.
- `domain` is required.
- `scope` is optional — concrete paths or systems the commitment touches.
